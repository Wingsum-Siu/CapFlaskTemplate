...so what is social anxiety?

Case Study: Negative Thoughts

Self-defeating subconscious negative thoughts can shatter one's confidence. Social anxiety can be the cause of the irrational thinking.

Moreover, research reveals that adolescents are more sensitive to the thoughts and impressions of others. Negative opinions become more significant and mean more.

The habit of identifying and eradicating those irrational self-depricating thoughts can aid in easing the anxiety. Your thoughts aren't always a perfect representation of reality, and you are awesome!

We are our own worst critics after all.
